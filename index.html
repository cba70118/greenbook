Ownership Leverage

High ownership = chalk. Low ownership + high projection = contrarian leverage for GPPs.

In golf GPPs, fading chalk (high-ownership players) and pivoting to lower-owned players with similar projections creates differentiation. Look for 10%+ ownership gaps at similar salary tiers. Stack by tee time wave when weather creates AM/PM scoring splits.

Course Fit as DFS Edge

Our composite rankings apply to DFS. A top-10 course-fit player at minimum salary is a DFS gem.

Sort by our composite rank, then cross-reference with DK salary. Players ranked in our top 20 but priced below $8,000 are the value targets. The same weakness-masked analysis that finds betting value identifies DFS leverage.
