Odds Source Notes

Odds are pulled from DataGolf's aggregated feed which covers Pinnacle, bet365, DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, BetOnline, BetCris, and others. "DG Fair" is DataGolf's model-implied fair price using baseline_history_fit probabilities. "Best Book" is the longest available odds across all tracked books. Edge = our composite-implied probability minus the best book's implied probability. Positive edge = we think the player is more likely to perform than the market suggests.
